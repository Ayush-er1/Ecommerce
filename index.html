About DecoMatic Nepal

Established in 2017, DecoMatic Nepal has become a leading furniture design studio specializing in creating exceptional pieces that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal. We understand the unique needs of Nepali homes and design furniture that fits perfectly with local lifestyles and preferences.

Our team of skilled craftsmen and designers work together to bring your vision to life, using premium materials sourced locally and internationally. We take pride in supporting local artisans while maintaining international quality standards.

Why Choose DecoMatic Nepal?

Premium materials including teak, sal, and imported woods. Expert craftsmanship with traditional Nepali techniques combined with modern designs. We offer personalized service and guarantee quality that lasts with our 3-year warranty on all custom furniture pieces.
